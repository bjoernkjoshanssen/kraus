Kraus

Bjørn Kjos-Hanssen

In this project we formalize parts of the article Unbounded length minimal synchronizing words for quantum channels over qutrits and notions in quantum information theory.

In particular we formalize the notion of a measure-once one-way general quantum finite automaton (MO-1gQFA) introduced by Li, Qiu, Zou, Li, Wu, Mateus [ 1 ] in 2012. As an alternative to “MO-1gQFA” we refer to these simply as Kraus operator automata. In particular we formalize cut-point languages as in [ 2 ] .
